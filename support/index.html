Frequently asked questions

How Emperorishop serves customers in Ghana—shipping, returns, and everyday support.

Where does Emperorishop ship, and how long does delivery take?

We ship to addresses across Ghana. In-stock items typically leave our warehouse within 1–2 business days; delivery time depends on your region and courier. You will receive tracking details by email or SMS when your order ships.

What are your store hours and locations?

Hours vary by location. Use “Find an Emperorishop location” in the footer to see current opening times, public holiday closures, and whether appointments are required for certain services.

What is your return and refund policy?

Most new, unopened products can be returned within 14 days of delivery for a refund or exchange, subject to our Terms of Sale. Opened items may qualify when there is a verified defect or an error on our part. Bring your proof of purchase and original packaging when returning in store.

How does trade-in work?

Trade-in estimates are based on device model, condition, and function. Credit can be applied toward a new purchase at checkout or in store. We may adjust the final value after inspection. See the trade-in page for eligible devices and current promotions.

Do you offer repairs and warranty service?

Yes. We perform in-warranty repairs through authorized channels and can help diagnose out-of-warranty options. Have your serial number and purchase details ready when you contact support or visit a location.

What payment options are available?

We accept major cards, mobile money where supported, and Emperorishop Installments on qualifying orders. Carrier financing may be available for eligible phone purchases—terms are shown at checkout.

How can I get help with a billing or subscription question?

For order receipts, duplicate invoices, or unexpected charges, contact us with your order number. For subscription services billed through Emperorishop, we can explain renewal dates, cancellations, and refunds according to the provider’s rules.
